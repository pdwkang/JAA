Dayoon Jeong

Managing Partner

Dayoon Jeong was admitted to the State Bar of Georgia in 2013. She received her undergraduate degree from the University of Georgia and her law degree from John Marshall Law School. Her practice focuses on personal injury, real estate closing, and commercial litigation. She has successfully represented individuals that have been involved in car accidents and advised buyers and sellers in real estate transactions. She is a member of the American Bar Association and Korean American Bar Association.
